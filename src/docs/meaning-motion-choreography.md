# Meaning Motion Choreography

Status: FROZEN

---

# Purpose

Motion in Meaning does not reveal content.

It guides attention.

The visitor should never become aware of animation.

They should only become aware of changing perception.

Every movement exists to support contemplation.

Stillness is preferred whenever motion does not strengthen understanding.

---

# Motion Philosophy

Craft expressed physical effort.

Meaning expresses mental stillness.

Movement decreases.

Attention increases.

Nothing performs.

Everything settles.

---

# Overall Rhythm

The chapter follows one continuous attention flow.

Transitions should overlap emotionally rather than visually.

No sequence should feel isolated.

# Forbidden Motion

Do not bounce.

Do not overshoot.

Do not stack multiple reveals.

Do not animate everything.

Do not compete with reading.

Do not create theatrical transitions.

Do not use animation to compensate for weak composition.

---

# Success Criteria

Visitors rarely notice the animations.

Visitors instinctively slow down.

Attention moves exactly where the narrative intends.

Motion disappears behind the story.

The transition into Living Heritage feels inevitable.
