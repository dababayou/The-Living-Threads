# Competition Integration Freeze
## The Living Threads

---

## Status

APPROVED

This document defines the final competition integration strategy for *The Living Threads*.

Following the evaluation documented in *Competition Integration Exploration*, the project will adopt **Approach B — Framed Documentary**.

This decision is final.

Future implementation should follow this document rather than revisiting alternative approaches.

---

# Objective

The documentary experience is complete.

The remaining objective is not to redesign it.

Instead, the goal is to ensure that visitors—and competition judges—immediately understand that they are entering the public-facing experience of a digital initiative dedicated to preserving Sumba Ikat.

The documentary remains the primary experience.

The surrounding context provides orientation.

---

# Design Principle

The documentary must never become promotional.

Likewise, the branding must never compete with the documentary.

Instead:

Branding introduces.

The documentary convinces.

Reflection remains.

The relationship is sequential rather than simultaneous.

---

# Positioning

The Living Threads is presented as the flagship storytelling experience of a broader digital cultural initiative.

The initiative exists to strengthen appreciation, awareness, and long-term preservation of Sumba Ikat through contemporary digital experiences.

The documentary serves as its primary public introduction.

Visitors should feel that they are entering a carefully curated experience rather than consuming marketing material.

---

# Integration Strategy

The initiative should only appear in places where orientation naturally belongs.

It should never interrupt the narrative itself.

Therefore:

• The introduction may establish the initiative.

• The documentary remains untouched.

• The conclusion may gently reconnect the experience to the initiative.

Nothing between these two moments should change.

The six documentary chapters remain structurally identical.

---

# Hero Integration

Before the documentary begins, visitors should understand three things.

First,

they are entering a digital initiative.

Second,

the initiative exists to preserve and celebrate Sumba Ikat.

Third,

the experience they are about to enter is intentional and should be explored slowly.

The introduction should remain concise.

It exists only to provide context.

It should never summarize the documentary.

---

# Documentary Preservation

The following elements are frozen and must not change.

• Story sequence

• Chapter order

• Layout architecture

• Motion choreography

• Editorial pacing

• Emotional progression

• Typography hierarchy

• Optical language

• Scroll rhythm

Competition alignment must never alter any of these systems.

---

# Hope Integration

After the documentary concludes, the initiative may be quietly acknowledged again.

This is not a call-to-action.

It is a contextual reminder.

Visitors leave understanding that the experience belongs to a living effort rather than existing as an isolated artwork.

The emotional release established by Hope must remain intact.

---

# User Journey

Visitor arrives.

↓

Understands the initiative.

↓

Begins the documentary.

↓

Observes.

↓

Reflects.

↓

Encounters living heritage.

↓

Experiences release.

↓

Recognizes the broader initiative.

↓

Leaves carrying reflection.

The documentary remains the center of gravity throughout the journey.

---

# Competition Alignment

This strategy strengthens every evaluation category simultaneously.

Theme

The digital initiative becomes immediately understandable.

Visual Design

The editorial identity remains completely intact.

Innovation

The project distinguishes itself by positioning emotional storytelling as the branding strategy itself.

Functionality

Visitors understand both the initiative and the experience without requiring explanatory interface patterns.

Responsive Design

Unaffected.

---

# Constraints

The implementation must not introduce:

• promotional banners

• marketing sections

• feature grids

• pricing-style layouts

• product cards

• testimonial blocks

• FAQ sections

• conversion-oriented landing page patterns

• intrusive navigation

• visual interruptions

The website must continue behaving as a documentary.

---

# Success Criteria

The implementation succeeds when visitors can accurately answer three questions after leaving.

What is this?

A digital initiative dedicated to preserving Sumba Ikat.

What did I experience?

An editorial documentary.

What do I remember?

The people, the craft, and the feeling—not the interface.

If all three answers coexist, the competition integration is successful.

---

# Implementation Scope

The implementation should affect only the framing of the experience.

The documentary itself remains unchanged.

Estimated impact:

Approximately five to ten percent of the interface.

Approximately zero percent of the documentary narrative.
